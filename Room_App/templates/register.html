About



Dental health education can have a positive impact on people's attitudes towards their dental health. The problem with giving recommendations and advice is that people often ignore this and can be resistant to change.

Dentobot is a conversational system that is curently under development. The purpose is to motivate and help people to identify their own dental health goals and help negotiate behavioural change along with general health goals focusing on prevention.

Dentobot needs your help. By honestly answering the survey you can help it's developers and researchers bulid an agent that can change people's lives for the better.
